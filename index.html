About Us

Jabs Electrical and Maitenance is a new start-up electrical company, found and owned by Jeffrey Jabulani Mazibuko(100% shareholder) with over 10 Years experience as a qualified specialist electrician.

Found in 2015, Jabs Electrical and Maitenance is a black run and controlled South African Company, it is also BEE complaint company in terms of HID determination. The company provides services to a wide range of clientele within the public, & private sector.

Jabulani who is driven by passion and new challenges in the electrical field possesses an advanced certificate in Project Management , Electrical Trade Certificate and Other Technical Training. He Started working as a Shift Electrician and all the way up to being a Specialist Electrician working in a variety of industries namely: Petrochemical, Construction , Manufacturing, Renewables Energy and FMCG.

With a high level of technical experience and expertise working in different industries, the services offered are surely of high quality and guaranteed high efficiency
